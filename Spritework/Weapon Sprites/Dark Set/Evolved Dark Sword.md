Slash example: 
A slash is an arc shaped AOE that propagates in the direction of the swing for a few 
frames; it is short lived and can hit any number of enemies. It typically appears at a fixed 
range from the caster. Note that the arc need not be a circle arc. 
Preferrably has a few frames of animation, but not required.
