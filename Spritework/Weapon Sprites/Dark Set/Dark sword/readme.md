## Attack 1
Type: Slash
<br>
Shape: An arc, stretched in the away from the caster to be narrow. 
<br>
Color(s): Purple, possibly with a gradient to black at the edges
<br>
Needs Animation?: ~3 frames showing propagation of swing from left to right (or right to left)
Description: The motivation behind the thin slash attacks is that the weapon is a katana, 
or some other long and thin blade
<br>
<br>

Slash example: 
A slash is an arc shaped AOE that propagates in the direction of the swing for a few 
frames; it is short lived and can hit any number of enemies. It typically appears at a fixed 
range from the caster. Note that the arc need not be a circle arc. 
Preferrably has a few frames of animation, but not required.
