## Weapon Details
Name: Heaven's Gate
<br>
Origin: [An EBF weapon](https://epicbattlefantasy.fandom.com/wiki/Heaven%27s_Gate)<br>
[Given the Night's Edge Terraria reference, this stands in for the True Excalibur](https://terraria.fandom.com/wiki/True_Excalibur)<br>
[The split into two smaller swords is a reference to Kayle's sword]([Kayle's Sword](https://universe.leagueoflegends.com/en_GB/story/champion/kayle/)
<br>
Similar weapons: [Kayle's waves of holy fire](https://youtu.be/sfXsAbSPRSk?si=PCrkFlwh8DyAMDtF&t=242)
<br>
Dps: high
<br>
Attack speed: high
<br>
Status Effects: burn
<br>
Description: Evolved Heaven’s Gate. A reference to Kayle’s sword when split in two. 
Attacks fire two slashes at once, mirrored, at higher speed and narrower hitbox than 
Heaven’s Gate. Fires waves of holy fire that burn enemies in a line.

## Attack 2
Type: Projectile
<br>
Shape: Arc
<br>
Color(s): White, yellow, orange, and/or golden.
<br>
Needs Animation?: No
<br>
Description: A wave of holy fire that expands and propagates away from the caster, igniting 
enemies.
<br>
<br>

Slash example: 
A slash is an arc shaped AOE that propagates in the direction of the swing for a few 
frames; it is short lived and can hit any number of enemies. It typically appears at a fixed 
range from the caster. Note that the arc need not be a circle arc. 
Preferrably has a few frames of animation, but not required.
