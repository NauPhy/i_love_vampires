Name for game: Cut and Gun
	*Pun of cut and run
	*The primary image used to market the game (the centerpiece of most Steam images) could be a serious looking woman with a gun
	in one hand and a sword in the other, commissioned
	*Cons: Cut has a bit of a self harm connotation, and the game will most certainly feature self harm. Not only might this cause
	subconscious prejudice from customers, it may lead to the game getting censored by various promotion algorithms.

Note: Even if the name sucks I like this sytem.
Gameplay theme- Cut and Gun
	* Playing off the working title, most or all characters could have exactly 1 sword and 1 gun. Actives and possibly passives would then be
	"slotted" into the weapons, perhaps as gems.
	* The primary art for the game could feature gems in the weapons prominently.
	* There's a lot of different avenues you could go with this:
		-Current favorite: Every active has two forms- a sword form and a gun form. The sword form tends to be shorter range but better in some 
		other way. You can choose where to slot it when you pick it up. Each weapon only has 3 slots, so you really need to use both.
		-When you evolve the weapon, you can choose a new slot
		-Each slot may give a specific bonus, e.g. sword slot 1 has +10% damage and sword slot 2 has +10% attack speed, and you can slot any 
		active into either one.
		-Sword and gun can also give special bonuses to all their slots
		-Instead of choosing your weapons at the start, you can choose from a random subset early on, like equipment in Nova Drift or 
		Dooley cores. At the very start, you have crappy generic weapons, so every option will be a direct upgrade (like Nova Drift)
			-New weapons are unlockable through achievements and/or meta progression
			-Allow reslots when you upgrade/swap the equipment
		-Pitfalls-
			-No upgradable bonuses that are specific to the gun or sword
			-No achievements that rely on using specific weapons- other than the default set
	* Some ideas for playable characters that interact with this system:
		-Left handed- sword on the left, probably some other effect
		-dual swords
		-dual guns
		-one armed- equipment selection pulls from both pools. 
			-Could get bonus slots
			-Could get bonus stats (like one armed in Brotato)
			-Could get nothing and just be a challenge character (one armed in Brotato is already a challenge character tbh)
	* Some ideas for weapon effects
		-Bayonet - a gun that has 2 gun slots and a sword slot
		-Gunblade - a sword that has 2 sword slots and a gun slot
